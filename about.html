About Us

Welcome to slopegameio.github.io!

Hey there, game enthusiasts! We're thrilled to have you here at slopegameio.github.io, your go-to destination for a world of free, fun, and fantastic online games!

What's Our Game?

We're all about bringing you the best in online gaming entertainment. From the heart-racing thrills of Slope, Slope 2, and Slope 3, to the twisting excitement of Slope Tunnel and Slope Ball, we've got your adrenaline fix covered. But that's not all! We also serve up a diverse platter of other free games, including fan favorites like Eggy Car, Retro Bowl, Monkey Mart, and many more. There's something for everyone in our colorful game buffet!

Our Role in the Game

Think of us as your friendly neighborhood game distributor. We don't create the games (that's the job of the awesome developers out there), but we make sure these gems reach you. Our mission? To be the bridge that connects you to a world of entertainment, bringing joy and excitement to players aged 13 and up.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

We believe in keeping things simple and secure. No need for accounts, no need to share personal info. Just hop on, pick a game, and start playing! It's that easy. We don't store any of your personal data, so you can game with peace of mind.

Free Fun for Everyone

The best things in life are free, right? Well, that includes all the content on our website! Everything you see here is 100% free and mostly user-contributed. It's like a big, virtual playground where everyone brings their favorite toys to share.

DMCA / Removal Request

At slopegameio.github.io, we respect intellectual property rights and take copyright seriously. Here's what you need to know:

  • All games on our site are copyrighted by their respective owners and are embedded for your enjoyment.
  • Many games are submitted or suggested by creators, owners, or users who want to share their creations with the world.
  • We're happy to help game creators promote their games and reach a wider audience.
  • Most games on our site are mini-online versions, often including links to the developer's website, social media, or app store listings.
  • While we strive to respect all intellectual property rights, we may not always be able to confirm this for every game.

If you're a copyright owner and believe your work has been infringed, we're here to help! To submit a DMCA claim, please email us at ubg[at]slopegameio.github.io with the following information:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.
  2. An exact description of where the material is located on our website.
  3. Your full name, address, phone number, and email (official company details if applicable).
  4. Trademark registration number and office, if applicable.
  5. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  6. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you're the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.
  7. Your electronic or physical signature.

You can also request removal of ads from a game page at any time by emailing us.

We promise to process all removal requests within 7 working days. We're committed to playing fair and keeping our gaming community happy!
